Winter Solstice 2023

2022 version

Context

Starting 2018 VACFSS has moved away from designing Christmas themed celebratory cards to the recognition of Winter Solstice.
The communications department creates and coordinates the design and the greeting cards to be delievered to the community partners.

Reflection

This is the second illustration I've created for VACFSS. and it's a landscape piece made with Illustrator. After making a piece that focused on methodology,
I wanted to explore celebrating Indigenous dances and peoples.
Moving away from just using gradients, I began incorporating texture by masking the clothes to emphasize the atmosphere of the night and the fire. I'm proud to have been able to reference photos I personally took at the 2023 Caregiver Appreciation Event.

Social Media

I created a new formate and design for the materials released to the public, this year the content was not only shared to instgram but also to LinkedIn. A picture of our elder April was also included in giving credit.
