Reflection

As the first major illustration I have created for VACFSS and the a landscape illustration with illustrator.
I have faced with a learning curve bouncing from ideas until the last week of the design deadline.
I also "mastered" the use of gradient, a tool that I was previously intimiated by.
I felt great joy in getting approval from the CEO and designing the greeting card that would later be printed.

Starting 2018 VACFSS has moved away from designing Christmas themed celebratory cards to the recognition of Winter Solstice.
